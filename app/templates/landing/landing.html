Under the hood

We implement the state-of-the-art technology and algorithm to deliver the most precise matching possible. Here is how we did it.

Text Mining

For our algorithm to better understand the paper you upload, we created a tailored PDF text mining engine to extract the topics and idea from your paper. (Experimental)

Stack

We utilise Flask for easy web development and our own backend engine for our own algorithm. The structure is flexible and scalable, making us ready to embrace larger databases and heavier algorithm in the near future.

Data Source

Our database sources are from the official journal websites. We update regularly to ensure up-to-date recommendation in this fast-changing era.
